Clicker Pawry

A Black Cat Clicker Roguelite with Parry Timing

How To Play

🎯 Controls

Tap/Click on enemy: Attack (costs stamina)

Tap/Click with ring timing: Parry incoming attacks

Active Skills: Use the buttons in bottom-right

⚔️ Combat

White rings: Normal attacks - parry to avoid damage

Blue rings: Feints - don't attack during the first 200ms!

Red rings: Heavy attacks - MUST be parried

🎮 Parry Timing

Perfect (±80ms): Counter attack for 2× damage

Good (±160ms): Block the attack

Miss: Take damage

🏆 Goal

Beat 12 floors of bosses to reach the endless mode. Collect skills between fights and spend gold on permanent upgrades!
